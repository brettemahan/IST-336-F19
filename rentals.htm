Bicycle Rentals

We offer a selection of street and mountain bikes for rent. They can be rented by the day or by the week. Our bikes are high-quality and will not fail you no matter where you take them.

Rental Information

Bicycles can be rented daily or weekly. All our mountain bikes can be rented for $60 per day or $375 per week. Our city bikes can be rented for $40 per day or $250 per week. Helmets and visibility vests can be provided upon request for an extra $5 per day or $30 per week.

In the event of a broken chain, flat tire, or other minor issues affecting your ability to use your rental bike, Mahan's Bicycles will fix the bike free of charge.

For crashes and total bicycle destruction, we offer insurance for $10 per day, or $50 per week. In the event of total bicycle destruction without insurance or failure to return a bicycle, the renter is responsible for damages or the cost of replacing the bicycle.
